Grid areas can be used to map out your website layout!
Very easy to align them in accordance to your whims.
grid-template-areas:
      'header header'
      'contentleft contentright'
      'about about'
      'footer footer';
      header header:Here there are a max of 2 columns,
      And the header occupies the complete width.
      contentleft and contentright occupt 50% of width.
      about occupies 100% width.
      footer also occupies 100% width.
