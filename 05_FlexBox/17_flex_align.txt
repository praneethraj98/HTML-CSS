align-items and justify-content are used on containers, not on the items themselves (because they align all items)


align-items is used to align on the cross axis
align-items: center/flex-start/flex-end/stretch
  -> align to center/start/end of the cross axis


justify-content is used to align on the main axis
justify-content: center/flex-start/flex-end/stretch/space-around/space-between
  -> align to center/start/end of the cross axis
  ->space-around and space-between used to manage space between items



align-self used to align individual items
